Privacy Policy for Dev/End

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile game Dev/End (the “App”), published by Morkalork (the “Developer”), handles information.

Information we collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. We do not require an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other personal data.

Data stored on your device

The App saves your game progress and settings (such as completed levels, scores, achievements, and preferences) locally on your device using your device’s standard local storage. This data never leaves your device, is not sent to us or any third party, and is removed when you uninstall the App or clear its data.

Third-party services

The App does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. It does not transmit data to external servers.

Children’s privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Contact
